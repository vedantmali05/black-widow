About the Movie

Overview

In Marvel Studios’ action-packed spy thriller “Black Widow,” directed by Cate Shortland, Natasha Romanoff aka Black Widow confronts the darker parts of her ledger when a dangerous conspiracy with ties to her past arises. Pursued by a force that will stop at nothing to bring her down, Natasha must deal with her history as a spy and the broken relationships left in her wake long before she became an Avenger.
